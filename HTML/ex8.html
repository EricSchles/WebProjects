On the way of life

Living for the approval of others is not living at all.

Everyone has someone they look up to. A master if you will.

This person comes in many shapes and sizes and is specific to you.

You cannot let them conquer you though. You must see them as a collaborator in your life.

Only live for your own approval. It's the only thing that matters.

No one is perfect, not even those you look up to. We all make mistakes.

The sooner you can accept that, the sooner you can accept responsibility for yourself.

Sometimes you have to let it all go.

Just live your life.

Living with honor is never easy.

It's something you choose to do. It's never something you earn.
